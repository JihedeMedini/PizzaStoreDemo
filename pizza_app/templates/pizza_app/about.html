Our Story

Pizza Paradise was founded in Oslo in 2010 by the Andersen family, who brought together their passion for traditional Norwegian ingredients and their love for Italian pizza. What started as a small family pizzeria has now grown into multiple locations across Norway, each maintaining our commitment to quality and tradition.

At Pizza Paradise, we believe that great food comes from great ingredients. That's why we source the freshest produce from local Norwegian farms and fisheries. Our seafood pizzas feature the finest Norwegian salmon and cod, while our meat toppings come from ethically raised livestock from the Norwegian countryside.

Our dough is made fresh daily using a secret recipe that's been in the family for generations. We let it rise for 24 hours to develop that perfect texture and flavor that our customers have come to love.

Norwegian Quality

Traditional Craft

Our recipes blend Norwegian traditions with Italian techniques, creating a unique and delicious fusion.

Community Spirit
